# Idea Intake Policy

Ideas are welcome. Scope creep is not.

## Buckets

- Implement Now: Directly supports AC, low risk, reversible, small.
- Ask Human: Changes product behavior, pricing, security, data, or scope.
- Later: Valuable but not needed now.
- Reject: Conflicts with goal or too costly.

## Proposal format

| idea | impact | effort | risk | confidence | bucket | reason |

## Rule

Only Implement Now items can become tasks automatically. Everything else needs human decision or backlog.
