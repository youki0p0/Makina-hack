- `.jen/mission.md`: current mission and AC
- `.jen/tasks.json`: task ledger
- `.jen/assumptions.md`: assumptions
- `.jen/decisions.md`: architectural/product decisions
- `.jen/verification.md`: commands and results
- `.jen/ideas.md`: later ideas
- `.jen/handoff.md`: resume packet
